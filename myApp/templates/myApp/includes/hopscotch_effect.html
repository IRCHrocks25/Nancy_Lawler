Example

The Hopscotch Effect

AI adoption inside most organizations looks less like a strategy and more like hopscotch.

Teams jump from tool to tool. Experiments start and stop. Different departments solve the same problem in different ways. Nothing feels dangerous in isolation.

Over time, data fragments, accountability blurs, and exposure accumulates — quietly, without a single incident to point to.

The issue isn't innovation. It's movement without a governing frame.

What’s actually happening

Most organizations didn't set out to "adopt AI." They simply started using it.

It shows up in pockets first. Then it spreads—fast.

Marketing runs experiments.

Ticketing chases efficiency.

Operations moves quicker.

IT and Legal try to catch up.

Each decision feels small. The consequences aren’t.

Without shared visibility and accountability, AI use spreads faster than leadership can evaluate risk or impact.

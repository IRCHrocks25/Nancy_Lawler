What’s actually happening

Most organizations didn’t adopt AI.
They simply started using it.

AI use spreads unevenly—one team finds a shortcut, another copies it, and soon multiple departments solve the same problem in different ways.

Each decision feels small.
The consequences are not.

Without shared visibility and accountability, exposure compounds faster than leadership can assess it.

Where it starts

Common entry points

Marketing

Content speed, campaign iteration.

Ticketing

Pricing efficiency, forecasting.

Operations

Scheduling, logistics, coordination.

IT & Legal

Controls trying to keep pace.

The issue isn’t one tool. It’s many teams moving fast without a shared frame.
